{# ML explanation card — for your supervisor #}
How the AI Works

PCHIS uses a multi-label decision tree classifier that evaluates each vital sign independently against WHO-validated clinical thresholds.

Each node in the tree maps a vital to a condition flag (e.g. Fever, Tachycardia, Hypoxia). Severity weights are summed into a composite AI Risk Score (0–100%).

When the score exceeds 80%, the system flags the patient for advanced assessment — matching the clinical confidence threshold in evidence-based triage.
